Paneer Tikka Recipe (Tandoori Paneer Starter Snack)

Paneer Tikka is a popular and delicious tandoori starter and snack where paneer cubes are marinated in a spiced yogurt marinade and grilled. You can also make it on a stovetop tawa/skillet. This recipe includes both methods.

Ingredient List

  • Paneer: Use fresh, homemade paneer for best results.
  • Marinade Base: Hung curd or Greek yogurt.
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, onions (optional: baby corn, mushrooms, tomatoes, etc.)
  • Spices for Marinade:
    • 4 tbsp besan (gram flour)
    • ½ tsp turmeric powder
    • ½ tsp black pepper
    • ½ tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
    • ½ tsp shahjeera (caraway seeds)
    • ½ tsp crushed kasuri methi
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
    • 2 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
    • ¾ tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • Salt to taste

How to Make Paneer Tikka

  1. Whisk 2.25 cups thick curd until smooth in a bowl.
  2. Add the spices and salt listed above. Stir well to form a smooth marinade.
  3. Add diced vegetables and 250–300g paneer cubes. Mix to coat evenly.
  4. Cover and refrigerate the marinated mixture for 45 mins to 3 hrs (or overnight).
  5. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet or tawa. Place marinated paneer and veggies in batches.
  6. Cook on medium heat. Turn when browned. Remove paneer earlier than veggies as they cook faster.
  7. Press veggies lightly to cook the marinade coating well. Fry until golden.
  8. Remove to a plate. Skewer if desired, or serve directly. Sprinkle chaat masala and lemon juice.
  9. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon-onion salad.
